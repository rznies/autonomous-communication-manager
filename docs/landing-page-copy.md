## The Problem

### Heading

Most people do not have a message problem. They have a decision problem.

### Body

Email and Slack are not just full. They are full of tiny decisions.

Reply now or later.
Ignore or follow up.
Archive or keep.
Important or noise.

That constant sorting steals hours from your week and keeps you stuck in reaction mode.

Today's AI tools help you read faster.
They still leave you to decide everything yourself.

## The Promise

### Heading

This is the layer between you and the noise.

### Body

Autonomous Communication Manager is a personal AI agent that sits across Gmail and Slack and handles the first pass for you.

It does not replace your inbox.
It clears it.

It looks at every incoming message and decides:

- this can be archived
- this is low priority
- this needs attention
- this is urgent
- this should never be missed

So instead of starting your day buried in communication, you start with a shorter, cleaner list of what actually matters.

## How It Works

### Heading

What it does, in plain English

### Steps

#### Step 1

It reads incoming messages from Gmail and Slack in one place.

#### Step 2

It looks at who sent them, what kind of message they are, and how important that person is to you.

#### Step 3

It automatically filters safe, low-value noise like newsletters, automated messages, and low-priority chatter.

#### Step 4

It surfaces the messages that need human judgment and can prepare a draft reply for you when helpful.

#### Step 5

It learns from your corrections, so it gets better the more you use it.

### Heading

### Body
