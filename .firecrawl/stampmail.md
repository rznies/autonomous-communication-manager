Frequently Asked Questions

### How does Stamp learn my preferences?

Stamp has three powerful features enabling that enable it to learn your preferences: Memories, AI Labels, and Context Search.

Memories: Stamp automatically creates memories about you and your contacts after every interaction, allowing it to maintain your full context and learn your preferences. You can also create or edit memories about yourself or a contact, and Stamp will automatically use these memories for all interactions.

AI Labels: You can configure AI labels in plain English and specify what actions Stamp should take on all emails that are categorized into that label.

Context Search: Stamp searches through your inbox and calendar to intelligently understand your writing style and availabilities before drafting replies or scheduling meetings.
